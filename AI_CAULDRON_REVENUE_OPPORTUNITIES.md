# AI Instruction: Cauldron Revenue Opportunities

Use this file for AI agents working on monetization ideas or product expansion around Cauldron.

## Scope
- Capture feature ideas that can generate revenue without changing the on-chain protocol.
- Focus on wallet-adjacent services, analytics, routing, execution quality, and premium tooling.
- Do not mix these notes with implementation-gap work.

## Opportunity categories
- Best execution and smart routing
- Multi-pool split trades
- LP dashboards and pool health analytics
- Live alerts and opportunity detection
- Quote freshness / preflight risk scoring
- Internal execution/searcher tooling
- Paid API or premium analytics surfaces

## Monetizable product directions
- Charge for smarter routing or best-execution features.
- Charge for premium LP monitoring, alerts, or portfolio analytics.
- Charge for execution-quality services such as stale-quote avoidance or route optimization.
- Charge for API access to pool state, quotes, and trade planning.
- Charge for automation or searcher/executor tooling if the product evolves that way.

## AI working rules
- Do not frame these as mandatory protocol changes.
- Prefer features that can be layered on top of existing wallet/backend plumbing.
- Separate product value from protocol mechanics.
- Keep a clear boundary between wallet custody/signing and any execution-service concept.

## Output expectation
- When asked for revenue ideas, produce prioritized opportunities with:
  - customer segment
  - value proposition
  - required backend support
  - expected implementation complexity
  - likely monetization model
